/phoneNumbers

Phone numbers

The Phone Numbers resource lets you get phone numbers for use with your programs and manage numbers you already have.

Base URL

https://api.catapult.inetwork.com/v1/users/{userId}/phoneNumbers

Capabilities

Verb Path about
GET /v1/users/{userId}/phoneNumbers Get a list of your numbers
POST /v1/users/{userId}/phoneNumbers/ Allocate a number so you can use it
GET /v1/users/{userId}/phoneNumbers/{numberId} Get information about one number
POST /v1/users/{userId}/phoneNumbers/{numberId} Make changes to your number
DELETE /v1/users/{userId}/phoneNumbers/{numberId} Remove a number from your account
